Hi, I'm Jack Hyche!

My name is Jack Hyche, and I am a student at Liberty University, where I am pursuing a degree in Christian Ministries. I currently live in Auburn. My passion is sharing the hope of Jesus Christ, and my goal in life is to faithfully push the Gospel forward by serving others, making disciples, and living for God’s glory.

My Mission

To Faithfully Serve and share the Gospel is the word. I believe that Jesus Christ walked on earth and died for our sins and then rose on the third day. Our mission is to be the light of Jesus, helping people know him and deepen their relationship with him.  “In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven. Matthew 5:16 NIV”
