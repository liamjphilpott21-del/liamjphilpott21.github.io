3-Day Devotional: Be Light in the World

Day 1: Called to Shine

Scripture: Matthew 5:14-16

Jesus tells us that we are the light of the world. Light is meant to be seen, not hidden. When you live with honesty, kindness, and faith, others can see Christ through you.

Reflection: Ask God to show you one place where you can let your light shine today. It may be in a conversation, a task, or a small act of service.

Prayer: Lord, help me to be visible as your light. Keep me from hiding my faith and lead me to act in ways that bring honor to you.

Day 2: Living with Purpose

Scripture: Philippians 2:14-15

Paul encourages us to do everything without complaining, so we can be blameless and pure. When we live intentionally, our lives reflect God’s goodness, and we become a glowing witness in a dark world.

Reflection: Consider how your habits, words, and attitudes either reflect light or darkness. What changes can you make to live with more purpose?

Prayer: Father, give me a heart that seeks to honor you in every moment. Help me to live in a way that points others to your truth.

Day 3: Sharing the Light

Scripture: Matthew 5:16

Our good deeds should draw praise not to ourselves, but to God. Light is most powerful when it reaches others. Share compassion, forgiveness, and hope so others can see God at work.

Reflection: Think of someone who needs encouragement or a kind word. How can you share Christ’s light with them today?

Prayer: Jesus, use me to bring your light into someone else’s life. Help me to be generous, gentle, and faithful in every opportunity.
